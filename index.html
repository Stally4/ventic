FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What type of projects do you take on?

We are focused on web design, development, SEO strategy and mobile app design. We help our clients figure out what they need and offer our best advice on a given project.

What is your hourly rate?

We only charge hourly for our ongoing projects that need work on the regular. The price for one-time projects is always stated upfront. Our hourly rate is $100.

What is the typical timeline for a project?

Depends on the scope of the project, really. Some projects take less than a week. Some may take over a month. The best way to find out is to get on a quick call with us or send us an e-mail and discuss it. No strings attached!

How do you charge for projects ?

We quote a price upfront so that you know exactly what you're paying and for what, and there are no surprises later. The exact cost of your project depends on the scope and requirements. A portion of the final sum will be charged upfront to cover development costs.

What metrics do you use to measure success?

The best metrics are customer adoption, happiness, task success, and engagement. Analytics can be crucial to understanding your customers and we would love to help you with them.

What if I need help after the project is complete?

We always make sure to help out our clients one month after the project ends, for free. For any help post that, we can work out an ongoing arrangement!
